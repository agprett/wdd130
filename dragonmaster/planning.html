Bandit-1

HP: 25

Effects: stunned, prone, poisoned

This is a tracker card. On it is useful information during a combat encounter. It has the name, hp, effects and some other useful things fro each creature in the fight. To add or subtract damage, type the amount into the input, then hit the + or - to add or subtract. You can also see status effects. To remove one, click on the effect. To add, use the dropdown and then hit the add effect button. You can click the remove button to delete the tracker for one individual creature. To keep track of turn order, there is an initiavtive input. In the display below, you can fill in the initiative of each creature, then hit the update initiative button. This will go through and assign the turn order for all creatures that have the input filled out and then highlight the tracker for the creature whose turn it is. Use the next button to highlight the next creature in the turn order!
