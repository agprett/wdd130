About Me

Growing up I loved learning how things worked and that has driven a lot of what I do in life. Through high school, I was obsessed with cars and eventually aerospace engineering. After graduating high school, I started down that path when a co-worker introduced me to programming. I went through Devmountain's web dev bootcamp and found I loved not only understanding how computers work, but also that I could make them do some amazing things. I relish any opportunity to continue to foster that love and knowledge!

When I'm not working on coding projects you can usually find me gaming, bingeing shows and movies with my wife, riding my motorcycle, or out in the garage practicing woodworking. I love the outdoors and love to get out any time I can. Currently, I'm also doing a night school program through BYU-Idaho to continue my education in the computer world!
